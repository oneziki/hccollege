Student Facilitator Evaluation Guidelines

Introduction

Student feedback is very important at HC College. All Feedback is apprieciated and viewed with clear objectives to improve your learning experiance at HC College. Student's confidentiality is uphead and respected.

How to fill in this form

Answer honestly, provide supporting commments next to each indicator to support your response. After all three sections have been answered, add all the scores for each section, place in the section marked "TOTAL"

Categories Evaluation Level

1 = Poor
2 = Below Average
3 = Average
4 = Good
5 = Excellent

Supporting Comments

This section is where you support your response with examples and additional comments you want to make on your response to that particular question

Additional Comments

A space provided at the end of the form for your ideas on improvements and solutions, to enhance your experiance at HC College.

Student Information


Evalutation

1. The facilitator was well prepared and presented the course in a well organized manner.

2. The facilitator presented thorough/in-depth knowledge of the course.

3. The facilitator provided clear explanations of important issue and principles.

4. The facilitator was enthusiastic, creative and confident during lectures.

5. The facilitator communicated effectively in class and made good use of the time allocated.

6. Dicussions and participation oppertunities relevant to the subject was encouraged.

7. The facilitator welcomed questions and responded to them appropiately.

8. Were activities, assignments and mock exams enjoyable and did it assist with your learning experiance.

9. Were activities, assignments and mock exams marked timeously and was appropriate for group.

10. Pacing and timing was appropriate for group.

11. Visual aids were used effectively (flip chart, Power point, Diagrams, ect).

12. The equipment and additional learning aids were prepared and working.

Content Indicators (Study Material)

13. Learning material was clear and easy to understand.

14. Content was well organised.

15. Helpful examples, definitions, data/other were included as support to content.

16. Content was applicable to subject.

Impact Indicators (Value to your learning)

17. Learning took place and i would attend other lectures offered by the facilitator.

18. New insights and knowledge were gained during sessions.

19. Content helped me learn and understand concepts.

20. Participation was helpful and assisted in my learning

21. Learning outcomes were achieved.
