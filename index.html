HC College

HC College is a tertiary educational institute providing quality education by means of classroom based lectures and Supported Learning (correspondence). The Mission of HC College is to ignite the potential of all students to become leading professionals in their chosen fields. We are focused on service delivery and student well-being, therefore our facilitators are leaders in their fields, our staff are passionate about student success and student well-being is always the driving force behind any decision.

Computer Studies.
